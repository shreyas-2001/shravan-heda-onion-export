About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Onion Exports

Leading Onion Suppliers Since 1975

Shravan Heda Exports is a trusted supplier of Indian onions, serving key domestic markets including Haryana, Bengal, and Assam, while also supporting leading exporters across India. The company deals exclusively in high-quality Red and Garva onions, sourced from established growing regions and handled with careful attention to grading and consistency.

The business lineage originates from PGH Overseas (Parashram Gulabchand Heda), which entered the agro-based onion trade in 1980. Over the past 45 years, the group has developed deep market understanding and strong procurement networks within the domestic onion industry.

PGH Overseas is led by Deepak Heda and Kiran Heda, brothers with over 35 years of hands-on experience in the Indian onion market. Their long-standing presence has enabled the group to build direct relationships with onion-producing farmers spanning more than four decades, ensuring dependable sourcing and stable quality.

Established in 2020, Shravan Heda Exports, operated by Shravan Heda, functions as a sister concern of PGH Overseas with a focused objective ‚Äî to extend reliable supply and service to exporters, importers, and domestic buyers. The company emphasizes timely delivery, transparent dealings, and long-term partnerships. With a strong foundation in domestic trade and a growing export-oriented approach, Shravan Heda Exports continues to strengthen its position as a dependable onion supplier in India‚Äôs evolving agro-trade landscape.
